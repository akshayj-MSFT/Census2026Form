Critical Northwest 2026 Census Form

The Critical Northwest (CNW) 2026 Census aims to deepen the understanding of the community's demographics, preferences, and experiences in order to help inform future production initiatives and equity. Please fill out this anonymous survey once for each ticketed participant. If you fill out this form online before the event, you have successfully participated in the Census. Please come by the Census tent starting Tuesday to get a sticker from us. Thank you for providing us with data and helping the community grow!
